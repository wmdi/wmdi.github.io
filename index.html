About Me

I am Mengdi Wu (吴梦迪), a second year PhD in the Computer Science Department at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Zhihao Jia. I graduated from Yao Class at Tsinghua University in 2023. I am interested in machine learning systems.

Research Experience

I've learned a lot of exciting knowledege and obtained useful skills from every research experience, especially from my great collaborators.

2022.02~2022.08 I visited CMU as a research intern, where I worked with Zhihao Jia on accelerating distributed DL training.
2020.11~2021.11 I joined ARChip Lab led by Kaisheng Ma at Tsinghua University, and worked on designing architectures and schedulers for DNN accelerators.
